Frequently Asked Questions

How does sleep tracking work?

SleepUp uses Apple HealthKit to read sleep data that your iPhone automatically collects. The app analyzes your sleep patterns and provides insights about your sleep quality and duration.

All sleep data is processed entirely on your device — nothing is sent to our servers.

What health data does the app access?

SleepUp reads the following health and fitness data from HealthKit (read-only, no writing):

  • Sleep Analysis
  • Heart Rate
  • Step Count

All data access is read-only. We never modify or write to your HealthKit data.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Your data stays on your device. We do not:

  • Send data to external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Sell or monetize your health information

You have complete control over your health data.

How do I manage health permissions?

You can control which health data SleepUp can access:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Go to Privacy → Health
  3. Find SleepUp in the list
  4. Toggle individual data types on or off
  5. Or tap the app name to revoke all permissions

You can change these permissions at any time.

Why does the app need heart rate data?

Heart rate data helps provide more comprehensive sleep insights. By analyzing your heart rate patterns during sleep, the app can better understand your sleep quality and detect sleep stages.

This data is optional — you can disable heart rate access in Health Settings if you prefer.

Is SleepUp a medical device?

No. SleepUp is a wellness and sleep tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Information displayed is for general wellness purposes only.

If you have health concerns, please consult a healthcare professional.
