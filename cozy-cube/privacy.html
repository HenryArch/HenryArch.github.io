Privacy Policy - Cozy Cube

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

Summary: Cozy Cube is a free puzzle game. Your game progress stays on your device. We do not run any servers or accounts. The only data leaving your device is collected by Google AdMob to show the optional rewarded ads you choose to watch, and you can decline ad tracking when the App Tracking Transparency prompt appears.

Information We Store

Cozy Cube stores your game progress — such as your best score and in-game coin balance — locally on your device only. We do not operate servers, user accounts, or cloud sync, so this progress is never transmitted to us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Cozy Cube uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded video ads (for example, when you choose to watch an ad to earn coins or continue a game). AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, the AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Device identifiers, including the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) when you allow tracking.
  • IP address and coarse (non-precise) location derived from it.
  • Ad interaction and usage data, and basic device and diagnostic information.

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's use of data is described in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and the Google Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

The first time an ad is relevant, Cozy Cube shows Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt and asks for your permission before the IDFA is used for personalized advertising. If you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads and does not use the IDFA to track you. You can change this choice at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Consent in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, AdMob presents a Google-certified consent message (Google's User Messaging Platform) to obtain consent for personalized advertising before such ads are shown, in line with GDPR requirements.

Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

We do not collect or hold personal data about you on any server, and we do not sell personal data. Because Cozy Cube stores game progress only on your device, you remain in control of it. Requests relating to data collected by AdMob (access, deletion, opting out of personalized ads) are handled by Google under the policies linked above; you can also reset or limit your advertising identifier in iOS Settings. For any privacy question you may also contact us using the address below.

Children's Privacy

Cozy Cube is not directed to children under the age of 13 and is not published in a children's category. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Deleting Your Data

You can remove all locally stored game progress by deleting the app. To control ad tracking, decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt or change the setting in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and reset your advertising identifier there.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are indicated by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
