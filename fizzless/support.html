Fizzless - Support

Fizzless helps you quit soda by tracking the days since your last one — with milestones, a savings tally, and a calm craving breathing tool. No account, works fully offline.

Frequently asked

Will I lose my streak if I switch phones? Fizzless stores data only on your device, so a streak does not transfer between devices in this version.

I had a slip — did I lose everything? No. Resetting starts a fresh Day 1, and your longest streak is kept.

What does Fizzless Pro unlock? The full savings figures, every milestone past day 30, themes and widgets, and the complete health timeline.
