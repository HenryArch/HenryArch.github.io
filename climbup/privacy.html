Privacy Policy — ClimbUp

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

TL;DR: ClimbUp does not collect, store, or share your personal or health data. All data remains strictly on your device and within Apple's HealthKit ecosystem.

1. Data Collection & Storage

ClimbUp is designed with privacy as its core principle. We do not have user accounts, we do not use external servers, and we do not collect any analytics or tracking data. Everything happens locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

2. Apple HealthKit Integration

ClimbUp requests read-only access to specific health data via Apple's HealthKit API to provide its core functionality. We request access to the following data types:

  • Flights Climbed (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierFlightsClimbed) — To track your stair climbing progress.
  • Step Count (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierStepCount) — To provide context to your daily activity.
  • Active Energy Burned (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierActiveEnergyBurned) — To estimate calories burned during climbs.
  • Sleep Analysis (HKCategoryTypeIdentifierSleepAnalysis) — To display your sleep duration in the daily dashboard.

This data is read locally on your device to display your dashboard and progress. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or health information to outside parties. Your data is yours.

4. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Because ClimbUp does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, you inherently retain full control over your data. You can delete all data associated with ClimbUp at any time by removing the app from your device and managing your HealthKit data directly in the Apple Health app.

5. Health Disclaimer

ClimbUp is a wellness and fitness tracking tool, not a medical device. The data provided by the app is for informational purposes only and should not be used to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before starting any new exercise program.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
