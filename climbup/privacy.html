Privacy Policy — Climb Up

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

Introduction

HenryArch built Climb Up as a paid app. This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

All data processed by Climb Up remains on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Your health and fitness information is processed entirely locally on your iPhone.

Climb Up accesses the following health and fitness data types from Apple HealthKit and CoreMotion:

  • Flights Climbed (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierFlightsClimbed) — Read-only
  • Step Count (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierStepCount) — Read-only
  • Active Energy Burned (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierActiveEnergyBurned) — Read-only
  • Sleep Analysis (HKCategoryTypeIdentifierSleepAnalysis) — Read-only
  • Real-time Stair Counting (CMPedometer) — Processed locally, not stored externally

HealthKit Data

This app accesses Apple HealthKit data solely to provide you with health and fitness insights about your stair climbing, steps, active energy, and sleep patterns.

  • We do not use your health data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not sell your health data to third parties or data brokers.
  • We do not share your HealthKit data with any third party for any purpose.
  • HealthKit data is read in accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines and is processed entirely on your device.
  • We do not have access to your data — only your device does.

CoreMotion Data

Climb Up uses CoreMotion (CMPedometer) to provide real-time stair counting and step detection. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any other analytics platform.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app (deletes all local app data)
  • Revoking HealthKit access in Settings > Privacy > Health
  • Clearing app data through iPhone Settings

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights Under GDPR (European Union)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your data.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process data based on legitimate interest and user consent.

Since Climb Up does not collect personal data on external servers, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. All your data remains under your control on your device. For data-related requests, contact us at: yslong.aksskas@gmail.com

Your Rights Under CCPA (California)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information is collected.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the sale of personal information.

Important: We do not sell personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. Since all data stays on your device, you have complete control over your information.

For CCPA-related requests, contact us at: yslong.aksskas@gmail.com
