Privacy Policy

FastUp — Intermittent Fasting Timer

FastUp collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking.

Data Storage

All your data — fasting sessions, weight entries, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (UserDefaults with App Group). Nothing is transmitted to any server.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health or fitness data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data

No Third-Party Services

FastUp does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no network requests made by the app.

Apple Watch

If you use FastUp on Apple Watch, data is shared between your iPhone and Watch through a local App Group container. This data never leaves your devices.

Children's Privacy

FastUp does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. Given that our core principle is "zero data collection," meaningful changes are unlikely.
